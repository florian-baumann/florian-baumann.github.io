project: reverie - The Idea

Big Picture:
While scolling throug the internet the best way to spread your random ideas, you get while sitting on the toilett and thinking about life, was the r/lightbulb subreddit. There are no websites online provideing a platform to discuss, develop an share ideas despite the fact that ideas are the only thing mankind can evolve. So for me, looking for the next side-project-idea, this was a perfect start even if my vue/nodejs skills were ... weak.

The idea of ideas:
Provideing a pltform for users to exchange and develop ideas. - github - tree - hashtags

First Prototype:
But ... where to start? I decided to develop a simple first prototype by myself. It should be possible for users to post their ideas and vote on those ideas. Building the fontend was not that much of a problem. I already worked with vue in my last project. (only switched from Bootstrap to Vuetify because somewhere i read it is better)
The big part was the backend. The backends i wrote before were very rudimentary. No log in/authentication. No MongoDB. No ... But with the help from {z}Koder (who creates very good webdev tutorials) i somehow managed to build it.
